Detailed Description

Instances of this class encapsulate the marks in the simulator.

A track mark is a visual cue that allows the GUI users to follow an agent, or identify a place. To faciliate managing marks from external applications, external tracks are kept separate from internal ones, so that fiddling with the GUI won't alter what your application is trying to display, and vice-versa.
